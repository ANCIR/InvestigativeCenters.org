ANCIR strengthens African investigative journalism by improving the expertise, sustainability and production capacity in muckraking newsrooms.

ANCIR does this by focusing on "business of news" systems that are essential for professional media organisations. This includes investigative expertise, such as training and access to resources, such as Investigative Dashboard; producing cutting edge investigations in-house and via collaborations; building shared technology solutions and data resources, nurturing revenue strategies and business models, and creating opportunities for collaborative, transnational projects.

ANCIR's 16 founders include pan-African investigative newsrooms, from Madagascar to Mozambique. Membership criteria, exclusive to newsrooms, cover the entire spectrum, from the continent's investigative newspapers such as Premium Times, to small investigative zines such as Maka Angola; non-profit centers such as iCIR; specialist investigative undercover units such as Tiger Eye, among others.
